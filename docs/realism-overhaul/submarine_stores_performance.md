## Notes

- **Area of Influence** — for warheads: approximate lethal radius against intended target type. For mines: influence/detection range. For decoys/countermeasures: effective deception or masking radius.
- **Activated Lifespan** — time the store remains operational after launch/activation. For torpedoes this is endurance until fuel exhaustion. For mines this is the armed standby life on the seabed. For countermeasures this is active noise generation duration.
- **SUBROC / Sea Lance nuclear warheads** — lethal radius figures for nuclear weapons are highly variable depending on depth of burst and target depth; figures given are approximate.
- **TLAM-N W80 warhead** — yield was selectable between 5kt and 150kt; lethal radius scales accordingly.
- **DCD / Type 2066** — UK countermeasure performance data is not fully declassified; figures are best estimates from available sources.
- **MOSS** — once ejected, runs an autonomous programme of speed changes and manoeuvres to more convincingly mimic a submarine.
